What Does George Scorsis Do

George Scorsis is an expert in the marijuana market with a huge portfolio of experience and understanding about policies that must satisfy health department standards. He used his experience he gained at Health Canada, operating at Red Bull, in the drink market to shift to a medicinal marijuana company at Mettrum Health Corp as the Chief Executive Officer. It was a big shift going from recreational use to medical offerings, but was a lot more gratifying being able to make an impact in people's lives.

George Scorsis was the president and CEO of Liberty Health Sciences; a company that produces and distribute top-quality cannabis in Canada and other parts of the United States where cannabis has already been legalized such as the state of Florida. George Scorsis is not new in the highly regulated industry. Prior to managing a cannabis business, he was managing an energy drink and an alcoholic beverage.
